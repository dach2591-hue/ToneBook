## How to Use

1. Visit the app (link above)
2. Click "New Song" to create your first song
3. Use `[Am]`, `[C]`, `[G]` format for chords
4. Use `[Intro]`, `[Verse 1]`, `[Chorus]` for sections
5. Transpose with the +/- buttons
6. Export and share with your band!

## Add to Home Screen (iOS)

1. Open in Safari
2. Tap the Share button
3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. ToneBook will appear with its icon on your home screen!

## Privacy

All your songs are stored locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server. Your music stays private!
